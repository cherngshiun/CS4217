Ham and Cheese Sandwhich — recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 thick crusty bread
  • 2 tbsp myonnaise or unsalted butter, divided
  • 2 tbsp Dijion mustard
  • 8 thin slices ounces Swiss or cheddar cheese
  • 6 ounces of thinly sliced ham

Steps

  1. Place bread and add mayonnaise.
  2. Add ham and cheese in between.
  3. Heat over pan until golden brown.

Simple Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce

Steps

  1. Scramble eggs, set aside.
  2. Stir-fry vegetables, add rice & soy sauce.
  3. Add eggs together with previous ingredients and fry well.
