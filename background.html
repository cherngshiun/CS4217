Kitchen basics

Important foundations: safety, tools, and technique.

Safety first

  • Always maintain clean and proper hygiene
  • Wash hands before handling food
  • Use separate boards for raw meat and vegetables
  • Keep a lid nearby when a pan catches fire — do not use water on grease fires

Essential tools

A few quality tools make a big difference:

  • Chef's knife, paring knife
  • Cutting board, mixing bowls, spatula
  • Frying pan, saucepan, baking tray

Basic techniques

Sauté — cooking quickly in a hot pan with a little fat. Roast — dry-heat in the oven. Simmer — gentle bubbling for soups and sauces.

Glossary (quick)

Mise en place
Everything in its place, prep ingredients before cooking.
Al dente
Pasta or vegetables cooked until still slightly firm.
