Why simple meals?

Simple meals are an excellent skill for beginners because they teach essential cooking techniques, build confidence in the kitchen, and promote healthier eating habits. Learning to prepare easy dishes reduces reliance on processed or fast foods, helping individuals save money and improve nutrition.
