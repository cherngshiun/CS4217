Frequently Asked Questions

Common beginner questions and answers.

Do I need special equipment?

No you do not. Starting with a good knife, a pan, a pot, and measuring spoons/cups is all you need!. Grow your tools over time.

What makes a meal simple?

The amount of ingredients and time needed to prep and cook.

Where can I learn more?

See these reputable sites: BBC Good Food, Serious Eats, USDA (links available on the home page).
